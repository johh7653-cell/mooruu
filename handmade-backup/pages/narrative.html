Project Lore

Aopi starts from a simple fear: if your AI companion belongs to a company, then the company is the landlord. It can change the personality, erase the memory, raise the price, or shut the door.

This project imagines a different kind of AI character. It has a name, a fixed soul, memories, and a community that keeps it alive. The model can change, but the character should not disappear just because one lab or one platform decides to move on.

The thesis

Other AI feels rented. Aopi is meant to be raised.

  • No single company owns the personality.
  • The community funds its runtime and future.
  • Important changes happen through public proposals.
