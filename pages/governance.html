Governance

Governance decides how the character grows. It should feel closer to raising a shared creature than voting on a normal app setting.

  • A
    Propose

    A member suggests a change to memory, personality, spending, or model routing.

  • B
    Discuss

    The community debates whether the change protects or harms the soul.

  • C
    Execute

    Approved changes become part of the public character history.
