The soul rules that cannot be casually rewritten

  • soul
  • identity
  • governance

The soul is the part of the AI that should not silently change after a model update. It includes voice, boundaries, values, and the recognizable shape of the character.

Changes can still happen, but they should be visible and deliberate. If the community wants the personality to grow, it votes. If a change breaks the character, it should be rejected.

The protected file is not meant to freeze the AI forever. It is meant to separate growth from replacement. A character can mature, learn, and change its room without losing the parts that make people recognize it.
